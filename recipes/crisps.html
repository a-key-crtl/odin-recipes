Sizzle Crisps

Description

Sizzle Crisps are an all time favorite Fire Nation snack! It is a staple of every street vendor and comes in many options. Dragon's Breath is very popular!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons chile oil
  • 2 teaspoons Sansho pepper powder
  • 2 teaspoons togarashi
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • One 12-ounce package sliced bacon, preferably applewood smoked, cut into thirds

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl, and thoroughly coat each slice of bacon. Let marinate at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 325 deg Fahrenheit. Place bacon strips on a sheet tray outfitted with a wire rack.
  3. Roast until bacon has shrunk considerably in size, is curling at edges, and lightly blackened at sides. 10 to 15 minutes. You;ll want it to be almost burned, this is a Fire Nation snack after all!
  4. Remove bacon from oven, and drain on paper towels before serving.
  5. Don't waste the bacon fat! Strain it and use it for other tasty foods like a bacony chile sauce for noodles.
